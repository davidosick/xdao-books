# Create a DAO

## Step 1: Navigate to the DAO creation page

<figure style="margin-bottom: 20px; max-width: 500px;"><img src="/xdao-books/assets/images/createDaoButton.png" alt="Navigate to DAO creation page" style="width: 100%; height: auto;"></figure>

Click on the green button on DAOs page.

## Step 2: Fill in the required information

<figure style="margin-bottom: 20px; max-width: 250px;"><img src="/xdao-books/assets/images/createDao.png" alt="Fill in DAO information" style="width: 100%; height: auto;"></figure>

• **Name** – your DAO's name  
• **Consensus type** – choose how decisions will be made:
  - If you have equal shares among all participants, choose **Equal**. Tokens will be distributed equally among all holders.
  
  <figure style="margin-bottom: 20px; max-width: 400px;"><img src="/xdao-books/assets/images/consensusEqual.png" alt="Equal consensus type" style="width: 100%; height: auto;"></figure>
  
  - If shares differ among participants, choose **Proportional**. You'll need to allocate corresponding token shares to each participant.
  
  <figure style="margin-bottom: 20px; max-width: 400px;"><img src="/xdao-books/assets/images/consensusProportional.png" alt="Proportional consensus type" style="width: 100%; height: auto;"></figure>

• **Wallets** – add General Partner addresses
• **Avatar** – optionally, you can change your future DAO avatar

## Step 3: Create your DAO

<figure style="margin-bottom: 20px; max-width: 500px;"><img src="/xdao-books/assets/images/finallyCreateDao.png" alt="Create DAO" style="width: 100%; height: auto;"></figure>

Click "Create". Your DAO will be deployed on the blockchain.

**Important note:** Your DAO will be in Pending state until it's fully processed within the blockchain. After complete validation of the DAO creation transaction, you'll be able to fully interact with it.
<figure style="margin-bottom: 20px; max-width: 500px;"><img src="/xdao-books/assets/images/pendingDao.png" alt="Create DAO" style="width: 100%; height: auto;"></figure>
